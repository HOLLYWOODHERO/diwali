Diwali, commonly known as the Festival of Lights, is a Hindu religious holiday

The spiritual "winning of light over darkness, good over evil, and wisdom over ignorance" is symbolised by Diwali.

People get ready for Deepavali by cleaning, remodelling, and using diyas to decorate their residences and places of business.

People dress to the nines for Diwali, use diyas and rangoli to decorate the inside and outside of their homes, and hold Lakshmi worship rituals.
